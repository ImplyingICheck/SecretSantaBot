Simple secret santa bot that will pick people to give gifts to based on a role that you give to the bot

In order to use the bot, do >santa "ROLE"
Where ROLE (without quotes) is the role given to all the people that are participating in secret santa. NOT CASE SENSATIVE
The bot will the DM everyone within that role saying who they have for secret santa and to check the server for the rules and addresses of everyone

To run the bot do 
python "TOKEN"
in cmd/terminal where token is the bot token
